How to cancel MTN subscription

August 04, 2024
Post

To make life easy for users, most of MTN subscriptions allow auto renewal as long as you have enough airtime for the subscription. To cancel the auto-renewal or opt out of the subscription, you just have to migrate to another subscription. But some will need you to use a USSD to cancel. Learn how to cancel MTN subscription.

MTN products and services include a wide array of subscriptions most of which have validity period such as daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, etc. Some of these subscriptions need to be reactivated or renewed if you want to continue with the subscription. But there are some other MTN subscriptions that have auto-renew feature. This means that at the end of the validity period, the subscription will automatically renew itself as long as you have enough airtime for the subscription.

Now if you have a subscription that automatically renews itself and you don’t want it again, below are the various MTN subscriptions and the codes needed to cancel them. Find the list below the USSD code you need to cancel your MTN subscription.

How to cancel subscription on MTN

For MTN monthly subscription such as tariff plans, all you have to do to cancel the subscription is to migrate/subscribe to another tariff plan that you desire by using the migration code of the new subscription you desire. But for some other subscriptions like internet plans, calling bundles, etc. you will have to deactivate them with their USSD deactivation codes. See a list of these subscriptions and their deactivation codes below.

How to deactivate MTN subscription

There are some other subscriptions to that you have to deactivate to stop them from automatic renewal. They include subscriptions such as MTN Data Plans, MTN XtraValue Bundles, MTN International Calling Bundles, and MTN CallerTunez.
