Did you know that less than 2% of the global population has natural red hair? A mutation of the MC1R gene causes this color. There is a 25% chance of being born a redhead if both parents carry this mutated gene. Today, the world is lucky to have talented redhead actresses who have made indelible marks the entertainment industry.
