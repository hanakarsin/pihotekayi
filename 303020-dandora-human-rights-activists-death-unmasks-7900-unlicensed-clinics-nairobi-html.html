Dandora human rights activist's death unmasks over 7,900 unlicensed clinics in Nairobi

April 29, 2024
Post

- Nairobi County has unraveled 7,964 unregistered clinics

- Njiru Community Centre where activist Caroline Mwatha died is among the unlicensed facilities

- The report was tabled by Roysambu MCA, Peter Warutere, who chairs the health committee

- Some Members of the Assembly, however, dismissed the report saying the committee had done a shoddy work

The clinic in which Dandora activist Caroline Mwatha died has been listed among the 7,964 unlicensed clinics in Nairobi County, a committee report has revealed.

Njiru Community Centre where the slain activist allegedly sought abortion services was found to be among thousands of unregistered health facilities in the county.

The shocking report was tabled in the assembly on Thursday, April 11, by Roysambu MCA, Peter Warutere, who chairs the county health committee.

“There are 9,043 registered health facilities in the county and 1,079 licensed as per attached list as of March 29, 2019,” showed the report.

The report asked bodies mandated with accreditation of the facilities to partner with the county government to tame rogue medical service providers..

“The regulatory boards include Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentist Board,Nursing Council,Clinical officers council, Laboratory Board among others,” Warutere said.

However, Makongeni MCA and Minority Chief Whip, Peter Imwatok, was unsatisfied with the report claiming the committee had done a shoddy work and would not assure residents of Nairobi safety.

He accused the chair of leading a team that did not provide viable recommendations on how to solve to challenge.

“Only three clinics in every ward are likely to be registered this matter is serious and the committee should ensure the lives of Kenyans are safe,” Imwatok said.

The MCA for Dandora 3, Charles Thuo, claimed his ward had over 27 clinics operating without licenses and the county was silent despite raising the issue.

The committee chair, however, dismissed the allegations by the two leaders saying they lacked basis and were merely political.
