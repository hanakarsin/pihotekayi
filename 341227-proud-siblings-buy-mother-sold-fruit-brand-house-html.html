Proud siblings buy their mother who sold fruits to raise them brand new house

March 17, 2024
Post

- The mother raised all her four children and saw them through school by selling fruits

- To appreciate their mum, the four children bought a brand new house as a gift to her

- She raised a chemical engineer, a mechanical engineer, an IT systems operator and a lawyer

Four siblings have thanked their mother by buying her a brand new house for her struggles and hard work in raising them up to become responsible and morally upright adults.

The woman managed to raise a chemical engineer, a mechanical engineer, an IT systems operator and a lawyer, all while working as a street vendor.

Katekani Terrence Makondo took to Facebook to share the heartwarming story. He saluted all woman who hustle hard to support their children.

"Sometimes you don't need a formal job to raise your kids and give them better education." he wrote.

Social media applauded the woman for her sacrifices and praised the siblings for recognising what she gave to them.

Many people took to social media to laud the children for valuing their mum.

Facebook user Petra Labuschagne wrote:

"Single moms work so hard to do the best that they can for their kids..most of them..so may she receive many blessings and I hope all you kids that received such educations care for her in her old age as she cared for you."
