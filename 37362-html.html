A former editor with Monalisa Chinda's magazine sues the actress.

The plaintiff, Kelvin, who has been in a war with his former boss alleged Mona, as the sultry actress is fondly called, of slander.

Kelvin’s lawyers, Megafield Solicitors, frowned at Monalisa's statement in the interview and handed out a legal suit notification to the actress on behalf of their client.

According to Nigeriafilms.com, who contacted Monalisa's publicist, the actress and her team were aware of the lawsuit.
