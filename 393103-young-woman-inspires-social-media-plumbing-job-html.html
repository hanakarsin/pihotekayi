A young woman has inspired many people on social media with her plumbing job which she said she does on weekends.

An inspiring video of the woman was shared on Instagram by the user with the handle @cynthiashalom.

In the video, the plumber could be seen working on a kitchen sink and changing the tiles on the wall.

She also added some beauty to a cabinet which she refurbished.

While the work was ongoing, the hardworking woman added some playfulness to it so as to make it less tedious and more interesting.

@cynthiashalom captioned the post:

"Dedicating this vid to all the hardworking women. Keep grinding. God bless us all."

In her caption, she praised courage and determination as she said that good things do not come knocking at the door and one must go after them.
