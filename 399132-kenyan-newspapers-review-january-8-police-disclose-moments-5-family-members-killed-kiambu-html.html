1. Daily Nation

Police officers investigating the deaths of five family members in Kagongo village, Kiambu county have revealed they were left for the dead by people who knew them well.

The bodies of the victims, Nicholas Njoroge, his wife Wanjiku Njoroge who are all professional nurses were found next to their dead children.

In the neigbour's compound, the body of a relative and a farm worker was discovered lying lifeless in what the residents believed was murder most foul.

Preliminary investigations showed they might have first killed Njoroge's wife, son and nephew downstairs before they went upstairs where the man of the house tried to jump from the balcony to the ground floor but they caught up with him and hit him with a blunt object before slitting his throat.

Deputy President William Ruto has hit back at the Opposition over the failures of Jubilee government insisting they should shoulder some of the blame.
