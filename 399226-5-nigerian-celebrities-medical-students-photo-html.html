It's known that most Nigerian celebrities in the entertainment industry did not study entertainment-related courses, some actually went to the university and pursued medical related disciplines.

Tuko.co.ke has compiled a list of 5 Nigerian celebrities who studied medicine or related courses in the University.

1. Kate Henshaw

This beautiful award winning actress is what you can call beauty and brains.

She pursued Medical Microbiology at the School of Medical Lab Science, LUTH (Lagos University Teaching Hospital) in Lagos.

She practiced briefly before joining Nollywood.

2. Jemima Osunde

This physiotherapist turned actress oozes brains when she is in her scrubs and knows how to bring out the beautiful professional in her in front of the camera.

She is a graduate of Physiotherapy from the University of Lagos, and we are constantly in awe of how she juggles both parts of her life.

3. Dr. Sid

Sidney Onoriode Esiri popularly known as Dr. Sid is the first son of Nollywood icon, the late Justus Esiri.

And like they say, the apple does not fall far from the tree as shown by Dr. Sid who initially went to med school but later found himself in the entertainment industry.

The entertainer studied Dental Surgery at the prestigious University of Ibadan.

4. Kiki Omeili

This is another actress whose entrance into Nollywood surprised many as she is a medical doctor.

The beautiful TV siren studied medicine and surgery at the College of Medicine, University of Lagos.

5. Beautiful Nubia

Segun Akinlolu, aka Beautiful Nubia, is another doctor in the entertainment industry.

The renowned songwriter and singer graduated from the University of Ibadan with a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine degree.
