What is Crowd1?

Crowd1 presents itself as a high-tech crowd marketing business. The system works by recruiting new people from society to its business practice. The deal is to keep recruiting more members with endless money-making promises or ownership rights.

The concept of Crowd1 explained holistically

Lucrative deals lure investors, and Crowd1 packages are just that. The concept of introducing as many people to the scheme is what makes investors earn more. Besides your investment, any additional member in your name guarantees some level of rewards.

You are allowed to buy a profit share package and receive owner rights/profit shares. Below are the different Crowd1 packages:

White €99

The white pack is more often than not considered a starter pack. Its rewards are not as great as the rest but newbies appreciate its gains which include:

  • One-off profit share worth €100
  • Weekly streamline bonus up to €10
  • Residual bonus 5% of the company monthly profit

Black €299

In this package, members are not shy to risk their investments. They enjoy even better deals from Crowd1 Black’s pack. Here are some of its gains:

  • One-off profit share worth €300
  • Weekly streamline bonus up to €75
  • Residual bonus 5% of company monthly profit

How are Crowd1 rewards calculated?

After selecting your preferred pack, your benefactor will facilitate payment for the chosen package. At this level, newbies are advised to trust their sponsors throughout the process. A common question that arises at this point is, "what are owner rights Crowd 1?"

As a member, you will get additional free proprietor rights (Benefit Offers) each week on Fridays. Ideally, you should ensure you claim the benefits through the system. Similarly, your proprietor rights amass depending on the level of the package.

How to earn Crowd1 Commissions

Crowd1 pays its commissions through a paired remuneration structure. Positions in the paired groups are filled through the immediate and asymmetrical enlistment of members.

There is, however, no restriction to how profound a paired group can develop. Project profit capacity relies on the team’s efforts which are also virtually pegged on Crowd1's package levels.

How does Crowd1 make money?

One of the enticing motives for joining the scheme is the promise to become an instant mogul. The organization refers to itself as multi-level marketing (MLM) plan, where you market, sell and acquire.

These guarantees have effectively persuaded thousands of individuals across Africa to purchase enrollment into Crowd1.

As with every Ponzi scheme, individuals bring in cash by procuring a commission for an extra part brought into the platform. This implies that the more individuals you bring to the program, the more money you stand to gain.
