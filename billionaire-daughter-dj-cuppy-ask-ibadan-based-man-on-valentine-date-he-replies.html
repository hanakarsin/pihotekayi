Billionaire Daughter DJ Cuppy Ask Ibadan-based Man On Valentine Date, He Replies

June 30, 2024
Post

Disc Jockey Florence Otedola popularly know as DJ Cuppy has asked out an Ibadan-based man ahead of the upcoming Valentine celebration.

In a tweet seen by Naija News, the billionaire daughter asked a man with the Twitter handle A$AP Larry to be her 2022 Valentine date. She acknowledged their destination issues as she is currently in Oxford and he is in Ibadan and suggested that they should meet on Twitter on February 14th.

“Dear A$AP Lerry (@_AsiwajuLerry) Will you be my 2022 #Valentine ? I know you’re in Ibadan, and I’m in Oxford but let’s meet on Twitter Space at 7pm on Feb 14th for our first date”

#CuppyLove

Replying to the request, the man agreed to the proposed date

He wrote: “Cuppy Dat My Queen!, you know your wish is my command.”

In a recent development, popular Instagram comedian, Kevin Anagbogu, professionally known as Lord Lamba, disclosed in a post on his account that he has been dreaming about DJ Cuppy.
