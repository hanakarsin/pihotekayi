'Selling Sunset' Star Chrishell Stause Confirms Relationship With G Flip

By Pippa Raga

Updated May 6 2022, 11:37 a.m. ET

Netflix's Selling Sunset is not only a gift for viewers obsessed with real estate; it's also given fans an inside scoop into the stars' personal lives, including their romantic relationships.

When viewers first met Chrishell Stause in Season 1, she was married to This Is Us actor Justin Hartley. However, in Season 3, he abruptly filed for divorce and left her heartbroken.

After taking some time to heal, she started dating again. In fact, Season 5 of Selling Sunset follows her relationship with her boss Jason Oppenheim. Unfortunately, their love was short-lived and was over by the finale.

But don't worry, Chrishell hasn't given up on finding her special someone just yet. She's actually seeing someone new in 2022. Keep scrolling for the deets.

Who is Chrishell Stause dating in 2022?

Chrishell Stause is currently dating non-binary Australian singer G Flip! She confirmed the news to moderator Tan France during the Selling Sunset Season 5 reunion special, which aired May 6.

"I recently have been spending a lot of time with someone that's very important to me. Their name is G Flip. They're nonbinary, so they go by they/them," she said, "and they are an extremely talented musician."

Their relationship started after G Flip tapped Chrishell to star in their music video for their song "Get Me Outta Here." Given Chrishell's soap opera acting background, she knew she had to do it.

In the video, things become intimate between Chrishell and G Flip. Chrishell added, "It was so much fun; we had such a blast. Not everyone's going to be ready for it, but I think it's great. I think it's amazing, the song is amazing."

Chrishell Stause dated Jason Oppenheim for a few months in 2021.

Chrishell began dating her boss Jason Oppenheim sometime in 2021. However, it wasn't until July of that year that they shared the news with the public through an Instagram series of PDA-filled images from a trip to Capri, Italy.

However, five months later, in December of 2021, they called it quits. As Jason wrote in his post below, they had "different wants regarding a family.”

Selling Sunset Season 5 shows parts of their relationship.

Chrishell and Keo Motsepe were also an item for a few months.

In December of 2020, Entertainment Tonight reported that Chrishell was in a relationship with pro dancer Keo Motsepe. The two met while filming Season 29 of Dancing With the Stars; that season, Chrishell danced with Gleb Savchenko and Keo danced with Anne Heche. According to ET's source, "Keo and Gleb [Savchenko] are best friends. ... Chrishell and Keo are both awesome and felt like, 'I'm single, you're single. Let's try this.'"

Less than three months after linking up, Chrishell and Keo broke up in February of 2021.

Prior to dating Keo, Chrishell was married to Justin Hartley from 2017 to 2019. They began dating in 2014. You can read more about Chrishell's past relationships here.
