El Classico, Liverpool Versus Man City And Other Matches To See This Weekend

July 18, 2024
Post

Football teams will be looking to continue their impressive performances in their various domestic leagues after an impressive showing in the European League.

As the domestic club football action return this weekend, Naija News will certainly keep its readers informed of the most recent fixtures.

In Italy, the Turin derby between Juventus and Torino on Saturday is sure to be exciting. Il Toro, who showed grit in a 1-1 draw with Empoli, scored the game-winning goal in the 90th minute through Serbian midfielder Sasa Lukic. However, the Il Toro are seen as the underdogs in the neighbourhood rivalry.

In Spain, Sunday’s El Clasico between Real Madrid and Barcelona will cause the Estadio Santiago Bernabeu to shake. The first half of the season’s performances have undoubtedly given Los Blancos’ supporters reason for optimism as they strive to defend their La Liga league title. However, Xavi Hernadez’s Barca Team will be looking forward to putting up an impressive display after a disappointing draw against Inter Milan.

In England, all eyes will be on Liverpool and Manchester City when they lock horns at the Anfield Stadium. After losing to Arsenal 3-2, the Reds, who had a difficult first half of the season, are currently in 10th place. Jurgen Klopp’s men will be looking to return to winning ways after an outstanding display in their Champions League tie. While Manchester City on the other hand will be looking forward to giving their fans something to celebrate.

In Germany, Bayern Munich will try to get back to winning in the Bundesliga when they welcome Freiburg to their Allianz Arena on Sunday after sharing a draw with Borussia Dortmund in the der Klassiker. While the Bavarians are still in their prime, defeating Christian Streich’s forces will put them in second place and possibly reduce Union Berlin’s lead.
