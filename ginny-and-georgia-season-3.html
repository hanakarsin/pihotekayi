Renewed or Canceled? 'Ginny &amp; Georgia' Season 3 Is Happening But There's More

June 17, 2024
Post

Is 'Ginny & Georgia' renewed or canceled for Season 3? Season 2 of the Netflix original series was released Jan. 5. Will it return? Here's the scoop.

After a two-year hiatus, the popular comedy-drama series Ginny & Georgia returned for Season 2 on Thursday, Jan. 5. The synopsis for Season 2 teases that the mother-daughter duo picks up right where the Season 1 finale left off.

Only a few hours after Season 2 was released, we inevitably found ourselves in a show hole. After watching the 10-episode arc, which ended with a major cliffhanger, fans wonder when the series will return for Season 3.

So, has Ginny & Georgia been canceled or renewed for another season? Here's what we know so far.

Has ‘Ginny & Georgia’ been renewed or canceled for Season 3?

Ginny & Georgia fans can rejoice as the Netflix series has been renewed for Season 3 and Season 4! Although the streamer hasn't provided any insight as to when Season 3 of Ginny & Georgia will release, at least we can rest assured knowing Georgia and her teen, Peach, aren't out of our lives just yet. And we suspect the upcoming seasons will bring with them some crazy twists.

In May 2023, Sarah Lampert, the show's creator, shared a photo on Instagram of the renewal announcement along with the caption, "You guys don’t even realize what’s coming for you."

The executive producer described Ginny (Antonia Gentry) and Georgia (Brianne Howey) as “really dynamic, messy women.” And according to Sarah, their story is far from over.

“Season 1 was a coming-of-age story for both Ginny and Georgia, and I think Season 2 is a continuation of that,” she shared. “They’re both going to have to really get to know each other on a deeper level this season, specifically to set them up for where I want this to go in Season 3. If we get it.”

Sarah echoed her sentiments in an interview with Screenrant, adding that there were “carefully planted details” that come to the surface by the end of Season 2.

“Here's what I'll say. There have been carefully planted things in Season 2 that will fully make sense in Season 3 if we get to tell that story,” Ginny & Georgia showrunner Debra J. Fisher teased. I guess viewers will get to see what happens to Georgia following the Season 2 finale after all.

Given the somewhat unpredictable filming schedule primarily due to the ongoing writers' strikes, it’s hard to tell when Netflix will drop Season 3 and Season 4 of Ginny & Georgia. As per Hollywood Life, filming has not yet started as of May 2023.

For now, viewers can watch Seasons 1 and 2 of Ginny & Georgia, which are available for streaming on Netflix.
