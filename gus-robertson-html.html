Post

Adopted son of Jep and Jessica Robertson, stars of the hit A&E reality series Duck Dynasty.

Before Fame

His parents began the process of adoption in 2014 and went through two failed adoption attempts before they were able to adopt him.

Trivia

He made his television debut in the Duck Dynasty spinoff series Jep and Jessica: Growing the Dynasty.

Family Life

His full name is Jules Augustus Robertson. He has four older siblings, Lily, Merritt, Priscilla, and River, who are biological children of his parents.

Associated With

His cousins Will and Rebecca Robertson were also adopted, taken in by his uncle Willie and aunt Korie.
