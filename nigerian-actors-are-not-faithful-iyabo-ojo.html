Nigerian Actors Are Not Faithful

May 06, 2024
Post

Popular Nigerian actress, Iyabo Ojo has noted that she has never seen an actor in the Nigerian movie industry that is faithful in his marriage.

Iyabo Ojo made this claim during a live chat with popular OAP, Daddy Freeze alongside her UK-based colleague, Victoria Inyama.

The actress also advised women that are married to actors in the Nigerian industry to purchase a shock absorber because ladies naturally throw themselves at actors.

She said, “Men cheat. Victoria you should not be surprised, you know how the industry is. It’s very hard for an actor in Nigeria I’ve never seen a faithful actor. It’s a fact. Do you know how women, girls flock around these actors?

“Anybody that is married to an actor should always have…should go and buy what they call shock absorber. Because it’s normal, it’s very difficult. We always just pretend.”

This is coming days after Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie revealed the identity of his baby boy and his second wife online.

Edochie in a post on his Instagram account disclosed that he welcomed a child with Judy Austin also an actress.

He further stated that he loves his new baby the same way he loves his four other kids with his first wife, May.
