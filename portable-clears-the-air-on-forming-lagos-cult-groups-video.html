Portable Clears The Air On Forming Lagos Cult Groups [Video]

March 01, 2024
Post

Fast-rising sensation, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable, has cleared the air on a viral video where he claimed that he founded two notorious cult groups in Lagos.

The singer in the video which was eventually deleted on his account claimed that he founded the Ajah boys, One million boys.

Portable who spoke in Yoruba in the video said, “Open your ears and be listening.

“Have you heard of Ajah boys, one million boys? I established them.”

Moments after the singer was condemned by Nigerians over his link with the cult group, Portable stated that he was misunderstood.

He further stated that he never meant that he founded the one million cult group, but has over one million followers.

Portable noted that he is not a thief, adding that he has people and nobody should try him.

The singer who spoke in Yoruba in a video on his Instagram account said, “I was seated on my own when trouble came for me. When I said one million boys, I meant fans, not terrorists. Just the same way pastors have followers. I have followers; I am not a thief. I have people, don’t even try to come for me.”
