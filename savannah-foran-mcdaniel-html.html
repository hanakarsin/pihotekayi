Savannah La Rain - Age, Family, Bio

May 21, 2024
Post

Television and film actress who is best recognized for her role as Ash in the film Bosch & Rockit. She has also appeared in TV shows Dis-placed and Content, and the film Dora and the Lost City of Gold. In 2022, she appeared in 10 episodes of the TV series Surviving Summer. 

Before Fame

She trained for 13 years in dance, acting, gymnastics and singing. 

Trivia

In 2016, she made her television debut as Naia in the series Mako Mermaids. She has amassed over 10 acting credits throughout her recent on-screen career. 

Family Life

She has a younger sister.

Associated With

She has appeared alongside Luke Hemsworth and Isabel Lucas in Bosch & Rockit.
