Though no official cause of death has been presented to the public, Seth had previously struggled with substance abuse and even appeared on Celebrity Rehab.

Shifty Shellshock is survived by his three kids and a few past partners.

While no reports show that Shifty was married at the time of his death, he has been married in the past. His first marriage, in 2002, was to a woman named Melissa Clark; however, the couple divorced in 2011 after years of separation. They share one son, Halo.
