It's been many years since 16 & Pregnant and Teen Mom first debuted on MTV.

Now, many OG cast members have returned for Teen Mom: Family Reunion. Fans are wondering how old the cast is today, and how many kids they have.

Read on for details about each cast member, from franchise staples like Farrah Abraham and Catelynn Baltierra, to relative newbies Cheyenne Floyd and Kayla Sessler.

Briana DeJesus, 29

Briana is an alum of 16 & Pregnant and is now a mom of two. She shares daughter Nova with her ex, Devoin Austin.

She is also mom to Stella with another ex, Luis Hernandez.

Maci Bookout, 32

This OG from 16 & Pregnant and Teen Mom is a mom of three. Of course, as fans know, she and her ex Ryan Edwards share son Bentley.

Maci shares two children with husband Taylor McKinney: Jayde and Maverick.

Catelynn Baltierra, 32

Another OG from the MTV cast, Catelynn's story has touched countless fans.

She and her now-husband Tyler Baltierra welcomed their first child Carly as teens, and subsequently gave her up for adoption. They also share daughters Rya, Vaeda, and Novalee.

Amber Portwood, 33

Another OG has grown up before our very eyes.

Amber rose to fame via 16 & Pregnant during which the teen gave birth to daughter Leah with her ex Gary Shirley.

She is also a mom to son James with another ex, Andrew Glennon.

Leah Messer, 31

Teen Mom star Leah is now a mom of three.

She shares Adalynn with her ex-husband Jeremy Calvert. Her ex-husband Corey Simms is the dad of her twin daughters Aliannah and Aleeah.

Ashley Jones, 26

Ashley is a bit younger than other cast members.

The Teen Mom alum shares a 5-year-old daughter, Holly, with her husband Bar Smith. She suffered a miscarriage in 2023.

Jade Cline, 26

Like Ashley, Jade is 26.

She shares daughter Kloie with her partner Sean Austin, and in addition to being a reality star, Jade is a realtor and salon owner.

Mackenzie McKee, 29

Fitness trainer Mackenzie is also a mom to three kids with her ex-husband Josh: sons Gannon and Broncs and daughter Jaxie.

The reality star has moved on with boyfriend Khesanio Hall.

Farrah Abraham, 32

Farrah's story is one of the saddest from the Teen Mom franchise.

The father of her daughter Sophia, Derek Underwood, died just before the baby was born. She has since moved on in other relationships, and remains in the public eye, often paying tribute to Derek.

Cheyenne Floyd, 31

Cheyenne Davis is this reality star's married name. She is a mom of two, with a daughter Ryder she shares with her ex Cory Wharton, and Ace, her son with husband Zach Davis.

Kayla Sessler, 25

The youngest of the Teen Mom: Family Reunion cast, Kayla is expecting her third child with her boyfriend Ryan Leigh.

She also has a daughter Ariah, with her ex, Luke Davis. Her son Izaiah is from her relationship with Stephan Alexander.
